Cell Division Fun!

What is Cell Division?

Cell division is the amazing process where one cell becomes two! It's like a cell's way of making copies of itself.

Types of Cell Division

  • Mitosis: This is how your body grows and heals!
  • Meiosis: This special division makes sperm and egg cells.

Fun Facts about Cell Division

  • Your body makes about 300 billion new cells every day!
  • The fastest-dividing human cells can complete a cell cycle in about 24 hours.
  • Some cells in your body, like nerve cells, almost never divide after you're born.
