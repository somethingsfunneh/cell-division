Cell Division

What is Cell Division?

Cell division is the process by which a parent cell divides into two or more daughter cells. It's how organisms grow and repair themselves.

Types of Cell Division

  • Mitosis: This type of cell division results in two identical daughter cells. It's used for growth and repair.
  • Meiosis: This type of cell division is used to produce gametes (egg and sperm cells) for reproduction.

Steps of Mitosis

  1. Prophase
  2. Metaphase
  3. Anaphase
  4. Telophase
  5. Cytokinesis
