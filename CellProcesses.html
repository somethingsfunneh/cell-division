What is Cell Transport?

Cell transport is like a cell's delivery system! It's how materials move in and out of cells. Think of it like a doorman at a building who controls what goes in and out!

Types of Transport:

  • Passive Transport: Materials move naturally without using energy
  • Active Transport: Cells use energy to move materials against their natural flow

How Does Cell Transport Work? üöö

Passive Transport Types:

  • Diffusion: Materials spread from areas of high to low concentration
  • Osmosis: Water moves through special channels in the cell membrane
  • Facilitated Diffusion: Helper proteins assist molecules in crossing the membrane

Did You Know? ü§î

When you put a raisin in water, it gets bigger because of osmosis!

How Cells Get Energy:

  • Photosynthesis: Plants make food using sunlight
  • Cellular Respiration: Cells break down food to release energy

Put a leaf in a cup of water in sunlight. You'll see tiny bubbles - that's oxygen from photosynthesis!
