Cell Processes: Transport & Energy

What is Cell Transport?

Cell transport is how materials move in and out of cells. It's like a cell's delivery system!

Types of Cell Transport

Passive Transport

  • Diffusion: Materials moving from high to low concentration without energy
  • Osmosis: Water moving across a membrane from high to low concentration

Active Transport

Moving materials from low to high concentration using energy (ATP)
