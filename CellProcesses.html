What is Cell Transport?

Cell transport is like a cell's delivery system! It's how materials move in and out of cells.

Quick Facts!

  • Your cells perform thousands of transport processes every second!
  • Cell membranes are selective - they choose what goes in and out
  • Transport helps cells get food and remove waste
